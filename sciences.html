Science Courses - University

Unlock the mysteries of the universe through our diverse range of science courses. Whether you're fascinated by the fundamental laws of physics, the intricate world of molecules in chemistry, or the complexity of living organisms in biology, our programs provide an enriching academic journey.

Bachelor of Science in Physics:

Delve into the realms of classical mechanics, electromagnetism, thermodynamics, quantum mechanics, and relativity. Engage in hands-on experiments and theoretical studies, laying the foundation for a profound understanding of the natural world.

Bachelor of Science in Chemistry:

Immerse yourself in the fascinating world of molecules and chemical reactions. Explore organic and inorganic chemistry, physical chemistry, analytical chemistry, and biochemistry through a dynamic curriculum that integrates theory with practical laboratory experiences.

Bachelor of Science in Biology:

Uncover the diversity of life with a comprehensive study of genetics, ecology, microbiology, anatomy, physiology, and evolutionary biology. Gain practical insights through fieldwork, laboratory experiments, and research projects, preparing for a meaningful contribution to the world of biology.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science:

Embark on a journey into the dynamic field of computer science. Master algorithms, data structures, programming languages, artificial intelligence, and software engineering. Engage in hands-on projects, coding competitions, and stay at the forefront of technological advancements. At our university, science education goes beyond textbooks. It's an immersive experience that nurtures curiosity, critical thinking, and a passion for discovery. Join us on this scientific expedition and pave the way for a future filled with innovation and understanding.
